Contact Management System

Contact management software and CRM software are both related in that they help you organize contacts. A CRM is typically much broader in scope in terms of the functionality it offers. Although the exact place where a contact manager becomes a CRM system is not clearly defined. You should know that while contact management and CRM are different things, the two labels are sometimes used interchangeably. This is due in part to their similarities.
